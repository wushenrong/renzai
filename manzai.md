## Puyo Puyo

Puyo Puyo and all its other version with Manzai (Super, CD) uses a speech bubble
for its characters for dialog.

The bubble has a black background with a white outline, and is above the
characters

The tail of the bubble is always on the bottom edge and requires different
placement depending on where the character is on the screen and how wide the is
bubble.

## Puyo Puyo Tsu (2)

Puyo Puyo Tsu uses a standard text box for pre-battle remarks. There are no
text or animation in the game that would consider to be dialog text or
cutscenes.

### Super Puyo Puyo Tsu (2)

Super Puyo Puyo Tsu uses the same speech bubble system from Puyo Puyo.

## Puyo Puyo Sun (3)

Puyo Puyo Sun uses the same speech bubble system from Puyo Puyo for its
dialog but with a blue transparent bubble and minor adjustments for readability.

Sun also uses a standard text box for post-battle remarks.
